## 3. Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

### PT24.PRO Launches to Simplify Finding Local Service Professionals in Poland

**Warsaw, Poland — May 10, 2026** — PT24.PRO, a new platform connecting Polish residents with verified local service professionals, officially launched today. The platform addresses a common frustration: finding a trusted mechanic, plumber, or electrician quickly when emergencies strike.

**The Problem:**
Traditional methods of finding local services involve endless Google searches, contact forms, and waiting for callbacks. PT24.PRO eliminates this friction by providing direct phone contact with local professionals — no intermediaries, no forms, no delays.

**The Solution:**
PT24.PRO is a curated directory of service professionals across key categories:
- Automotive: mechanics, auto electricians, tow trucks, tire services
- Home services: plumbers, electricians, locksmiths

The platform is available in major Polish cities including Warsaw, Kraków, Katowice, Wrocław, Gdańsk, and Poznań, with plans to expand to 20+ cities by year-end.

**How It Works:**
1. Users select a service and city
2. PT24.PRO displays local professionals with verified contact information
3. Users call directly — no forms, no waiting

**For Businesses:**
Local service providers can create profiles on PT24.PRO to increase visibility in local search results. Unlike lead generation platforms that charge per contact, PT24.PRO uses a transparent subscription model — businesses pay for visibility, not per lead.

**Key Features:**
- Auto-location detection using browser geolocation
- Live activity feed showing real-time platform activity
- Mobile-first design optimized for on-the-go searches
- Smart search handling Polish characters and typos
- Direct phone contact — no intermediaries

**Founder Statement:**
"In 2023, I spent three hours trying to find a mechanic in Katowice," said [Founder Name], creator of PT24.PRO. "That experience showed me how broken the process is. PT24.PRO is my answer to that frustration — a platform that prioritizes speed, locality, and direct communication."

**Availability:**
PT24.PRO is live at https://pt24.pro. The platform is free for users and offers a 14-day free trial for businesses.

**About PT24.PRO:**
PT24.PRO is a Polish local services platform founded in 2026. The company is based in [City, Poland] and is committed to simplifying how residents find trusted local professionals.

**Contact Information:**
