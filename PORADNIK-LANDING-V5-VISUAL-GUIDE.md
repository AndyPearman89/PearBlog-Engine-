## 🎬 Animations & Interactions

### On Load
1. **Hero elements fade in** sequentially (0.1s stagger)
   - Badge → Title → Subtitle → Form → Trust signals
2. **Scroll indicator bounces** continuously

### On Scroll
1. **Stats counters animate** when section enters viewport
   - Count from 0 to target over 2 seconds
2. **Cards fade and slide up** as they enter viewport
   - 100ms delay between each card
3. **Testimonials lift** on hover (-4px)

### On Interaction
1. **Form submission**
   - Button shows "Wysyłanie..." spinner
   - Success: Green banner appears, form resets
   - Error: Red banner appears with message
2. **FAQ click**
   - Arrow rotates 180deg
   - Answer expands smoothly (height transition)
   - Other questions collapse
3. **Button hover**
   - Lifts up (-2px)
   - Shadow increases
   - Smooth 0.3s transition
