### Pipeline Flow (Hourly via WP-Cron) вҖ” 12 Steps

```
Topic Queue
  Step 01 вҶ’ PromptBuilderFactory (selects builder by industry/niche)
  Step 02 вҶ’ GPT-4o-mini content generation
  Step 03 вҶ’ DuplicateDetector (blocks if similarity вүҘ 80%)
  Step 04 вҶ’ Draft post created (WordPress)
  Step 05 вҶ’ SEOEngine (title, meta, Schema.org, Open Graph)
  Step 06 вҶ’ MonetizationEngine (ad slots + affiliate + SaaS CTA)
  Step 07 вҶ’ InternalLinker (up to 5 links injected)
  Step 08 вҶ’ ImageGenerator (DALL-E 3 featured image)
  Step 09 вҶ’ DuplicateIndex updated
  Step 10 вҶ’ Post published
  Step 11 вҶ’ QualityScorer (score saved as post meta)
  Step 12 вҶ’ AlertManager notified (Slack/Discord/email)

~55 sec / article В· $0.08 / article (with image)
```
